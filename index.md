<section class="content-section profile-summary">
  <h2>Profile</h2>
  <p>
    Zhennan Gao is an Associate Professor at the Business School, Central University of Finance and Economics.
    Her main research areas include corporate finance, investor behavior, and empirical asset pricing. She has
    published academic papers in leading journals including <em>Financial Management</em>,
    <em>Journal of Portfolio Management</em>, and <em>China Economic Quarterly</em>. Her work received the
    Top Paper in Financial Management award. She is the principal investigator of a National Natural Science
    Foundation Youth Project and has participated in two National Natural Science Foundation General Projects.
  </p>
</section>
